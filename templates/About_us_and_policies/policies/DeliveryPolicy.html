Delivery Policy 🏡

Requirements

Our Delivery Policy is very simple and easy to understand. To make sure smooth delivery, you need to provide complete Home Address where you want to get your order. It should be complete, including:

  • Province
  • City
  • Town / Area / Colony
  • Street Name / Street Number
  • Apartment Number / House Number / Building Number
  • Postal Code

After providing us your complete home address, you need to add your phone number. Because Delivery Partners can contact you in case they are unable to locate your exact location. 🚚

Important NOTE For Buyers

Whenever you place new order, you need to add new address for that order. Looks somehow discomforting? Don't worry, if you are registered user, we will have your Home Address. You can select your home address as your delivery address while confirming your order. Your home address will be sent automatically to delivery services! Easy Now?😌

We recommend you to please visit our Courier Services page to know about our courier services partner.
